Canonical first

Keep collection separate from training format.

Preserve structured reasoning, tool calls, tool results, and source metadata in agent_trace_v1. Convert only when a training run needs a target format.

  1. 01 Discover

    Scan known local stores without indexing your filesystem into a service.

    ath discover --root "$HOME"
  2. 02 Normalize

    Replay branches and retain structured agent events in canonical JSONL.

    ath ingest --manifest traces.jsonl
  3. 03 Audit

    Block secrets, deny patterns, and unsafe artifacts before release.

    ath audit --profile public
  4. 04 Render

    Emit OpenAI, Anthropic, ChatML, ShareGPT, SFT, or Ornith/Qwen data.

    ath render --format chatml

Local by default

Your traces do not need a new cloud.

Discovery, normalization, schema validation, deterministic auditing, rendering, and dataset packaging run on your machine. External review and upload happen only when you invoke those workflows.
