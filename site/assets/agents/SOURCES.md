# Agent mark sources

These marks identify supported trace sources. They do not imply endorsement or
partnership. Each mark remains the property of its respective owner.

- `claude-code.svg`: Claude Spark from the official Anthropic media resources kit, downloaded from <https://www.anthropic.com/press-kit>.
- `codex.png`: Current official Codex desktop app icon, downsampled from the `icon-codex-dark-color.png` resource bundled with the official ChatGPT/Codex macOS app. Product source: <https://openai.com/codex/>; mark terms: <https://openai.com/brand/>.
- `pi.svg`: Pi mark from <https://pi.dev/logo-auto.svg>. The adaptive SVG is pinned to its official black treatment for contrast on this site.
- `opencode.svg`: OpenCode logo from the official brand kit at <https://opencode.ai/brand>.
- `omp.svg`: OMP icon from <https://github.com/can1357/oh-my-pi/blob/main/assets/icon.svg>. Its light monochrome strokes are pinned to dark ink for contrast; the geometry and orange connector remain unchanged.
- `cursor-agent.svg`: Cursor cube from the official brand kit at <https://cursor.com/brand>.
